Behind the Scenes

We are not your conventional office. We don’t have timings and we certainly don’t do the mundane 9-5. Coding is at our heart and we love a hands-on approach. So, you code. Your Boss codes. His Boss codes. And maybe the office boy codes too. Here, Leaders are built on reputation and skill sets, not titles and designations. We let our work define us rather than our age. We’ve seen leaders as young as 24 years and as old as 50.

We are Indonesia’s largest & fastest growing tech unicorn. And we’re looking for people like you.

The GO-JEK Advantage

An employee can claim upto Rs.12000 annually towards expenses related to health and wellness. For example: Gym membership, buying a cycle, saunas and massages, sports equipment etc...

Books can be reimbursed for upto 6,000 and more for technical ones if required

Broadband and mobile bill will be reimbursed

Unlimited sick leaves - Because we trust our employees, because we have 18 products and employees are micro-entreprenuers owning the product

All employees are encouraged to travel to Jakarta to get a feel of the product, its use cases and understand what makes GO-JEK a verb in Indonesia

No office timings; the team collectively decides what works for them. No dress code, an atmosphere of independence in decision-making

At GO-JEK, we fail a lot.
In fact, we love failing.

Because we believe that failing is learning. And if we’re not failing, we’re just not doing it right. We are a global unit. An amalgamation of sorts. We have a cross-pollination of ideas from Singapore, Indonesia and India. Different cultures, different mindsets, unified in solving problems and learning along the way.
