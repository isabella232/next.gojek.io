Behind the Scenes

We are not your conventional office. We don’t have timings and we certainly don’t do the mundane 9-5. Coding is at our heart and we love a hands-on approach. So, you code. Your Boss codes. His Boss codes. And maybe the office boy codes too. Here, Leaders are built on reputation and skill sets, not titles and designations. We let our work define us rather than our age. We’ve seen leaders as young as 24 years and as old as 50.

We are Indonesia’s largest & fastest growing tech unicorn.

And we’re looking for people like you.

At GO-JEK, we fail a lot.
In fact, we love failing.

Because we believe that failing is learning. And if we’re not failing, we’re just not doing it right. We are a global unit. An amalgamation of sorts. We have a cross-pollination of ideas from Singapore, Indonesia and India. Different cultures, different mindsets, unified in solving problems and learning along the way.

Also, at GO-JEK we embrace chaos

Because you can’t build if you can't expect the unexpected.
